Probability of Dominant Phenotype

This app takes three positive integers k, m, and n as input, representing a population of k + m + n organisms. k individuals are homozygous dominant for a factor, m are heterozygous, and n are homozygous recessive. This app returns the probability that a randomly selected mating pair will produce an individual with a dominant phenotype.
